Cinnamon Swirl French Toast

Description

French toast. A staple for many-a weekend breakfasts or brunches the world over. It's a classic for a reason - it's simple yet decadent, and virtually perfect as is. This begs the question: is it possible to improve upon one of the OG breakfast dishes? The answer is yes, and this recipe will show you how with a little twist...a cinnamon twist that is.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 slices of cinnamon swirl bread, any brand
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. In a dish wide enough to fit a slice of bread, whisk milk, eggs, and vanilla.
  2. Dip both sides of the cinnamon swirl bread into the mixture, ensuring both sides are coated well.
  3. Set a large skilled on medium heat and melt butter. Once melted, add bread and fry until golden brown (3-4 minutes). Flip and fry the other side for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Serve with maple syrup, a sprinkling of powdered sugar, and/or fresh berries.
